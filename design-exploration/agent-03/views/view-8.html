Spaghetti alla Carbonara

FROM Seriouseats SAVED 3 months ago COOKED 7×, last 4 days ago

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 200 g guanciale, diced
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 whole large egg
  • 80 g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • 40 g Parmigiano-Reggiano, grated
  • Freshly cracked black pepper
  • Coarse salt for the pasta water

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Render the guanciale in a wide pan over medium heat until golden and crisp at the edges, about 6 minutes. Reserve.
  3. Whisk yolks, whole egg, both cheeses, and lots of pepper in a bowl.
  4. Cook spaghetti to al dente. Reserve a mug of pasta water.
  5. Off the heat, toss pasta with guanciale and fat, then with the egg mixture, loosening with pasta water until creamy.
  6. Plate with extra cheese and pepper. Eat immediately.

Notes

  • Best with thick-cut guanciale — pancetta is fine but flatter.
  • Tested: 250 ml pasta water was the sweet spot.
