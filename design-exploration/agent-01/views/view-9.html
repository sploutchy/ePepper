Bibimbap

from Maangchi - saved 6 months ago - cooked 6x, last last month

Ingredients

  • 3 cups short-grain rice, cooked
  • 200 g sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 200 g spinach, blanched
  • 200 g bean sprouts, blanched
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 zucchini, julienned
  • 200 g shiitake, sliced
  • 4 eggs
  • Gochujang sauce (gochujang, sesame oil, sugar, vinegar)
  • Soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, salt

Instructions

Banchan prep

  1. Season each vegetable separately: salt + sesame oil for spinach + sprouts; saute carrots, zucchini, mushrooms each on their own.
  2. Marinate beef in soy + sugar + sesame + garlic; sear hot and fast.

Bowls

  1. Heat stone bowls until smoking. Brush with sesame oil.
  2. Pack hot rice. Arrange banchan around the edge.
  3. Top with beef and a sunny-side egg.
  4. Sauce on the side. Stir at the table, eat where the rice crisps against the bowl.

Notes

  • The dolsot crackle is the whole point - don't skip preheating the bowls.
