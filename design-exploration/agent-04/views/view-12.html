Cacio e Pepe

from Pastagrannies · saved last week · never cooked

Ingredients

  • 400 g tonnarelli (or spaghetti)
  • 200 g Pecorino Romano, finely grated
  • Lots of freshly cracked black pepper
  • Coarse salt

Instructions

  1. Toast pepper in a dry pan to wake it up.
  2. Cook pasta to very al dente — 2 minutes short. Reserve plenty of pasta water.
  3. Whisk pasta water (warm, not hot) into the cheese to a thick paste.
  4. Toss pasta, pepper, and a splash more pasta water in a wide pan over very low heat, swirling, until creamy.
  5. Serve immediately. The dish does not wait.
