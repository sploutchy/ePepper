Flash device

Recovery path for when OTA can't reach the device — a bad build, or a chip that won't boot. Normally firmware updates itself over Wi-Fi; you only need this to re-flash over the cable.

Plug the reTerminal into this computer over USB-C, click Install, and pick the serial port. Needs desktop Chrome or Edge (Web Serial).

On Linux, use the full Chrome/Edge installed from the official .deb — the Snap and Flatpak builds are sandboxed and can't see /dev/ttyUSB*, so the port picker comes up empty even when the device is plugged in. Your user also needs to be in the dialout group to open the port.
